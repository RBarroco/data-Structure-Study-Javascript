   Key words used in job description;
3) Personalized

   GitHub, portfolio website

   Resume Cheat Sheet
✅ Use a pre-designed resume template
✅ Make the resume fit on 1 page
✅ Include words from job description        
KEY WORDS OF THE JOB DESCRIPTION.
✅ Include company name you are applying to  
NAME OF THE COMPANY;
✅ Does your first item on your resume reflect what they are looking for? 
ALIGN OUR RESUME TO WHAT THEY WANT;
✅ Experience titles demonstrate value  USING THE EXPERIENCE TITLE IN EACH JOB EXPERIENCE: 
(PROJECT MANAGER) FOR EXAMPLE.
✅ Do you have an online link? 
GITHUB, PORTFOLIO PAGE
✅ Remove the word “I”
✅ No buzzwords describing how great you are
✅ Are you using Action words? 
TESTING, MANAGING, DEVELOPING, ACTION WORDS;
✅ Measure everything in terms of impact, don’t just describe your responsibilities 
NUMBERS OF IMPROVEMENT; I DID THIS AND IT RESULT IN IT
✅ Technical Knowledge/Skills should include what they are looking for. Only show years if it is impressive
NO YEARS
✅ Include only sections/items that are impressive: Experience, Projects, Education, Technical Skills
DIRECT THE EXPERIENCE SPECIFICALLY TO THE POSITION
✅ No typos or bad grammar
